### a)
La corriente que circula inicialmente a $0\degree$ sera $$\begin{align}
I&=\frac{\Delta V}{R} \\
&=\frac{120\text{V}}{8\Omega}\qquad\therefore I_{0\degree\text{C}}=15\text{A}
\end{align}$$

### b)
Para calcular la resistencia a una temperatura $T$ utilizamos la relación
$$R(T)=R_{0\degree\text{C}}[1+\alpha \cdot(T-0\degree\text{C})]$$
$$\begin{align}
R(T=1000\degree\text{C})&=8\Omega \left[ 1+0.4\times 10^{-3}\frac{1}{\degree\text{C}}\cdot(1000\degree\text{C}-0\degree\text{C})\right]  \\
R(T=1000\degree\text{C})&=8\Omega \cdot 1.4\qquad\therefore R(T=1000\degree\text{C}=11.2\Omega)
\end{align}
$$

### c)
Podemos calcular la potencia del calentador como $$P_{R}=\frac{\Delta V_{R}^2}{R}$$
$$\begin{align}
P_{R}&=\frac{(120\text{V})^{2}}{11.2\Omega} \\
&=1285.7 \underbrace{\frac{\text{V}^{2}}{\Omega}}_{=W}\qquad\therefore P_{R}=1.29\text{ kW}
\end{align}$$
