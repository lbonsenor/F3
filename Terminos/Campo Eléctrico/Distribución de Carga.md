### Distribución de Carga Lineal
$$Q=\lambda \cdot \ell\qquad d q=\lambda \cdot d \ell$$

### Distribución de Carga Superficial
$$Q=\sigma \cdot A\qquad d q=\sigma \cdot d A$$
> [!info] Superficies $S=\iint_{A} d A$
>$$\begin{align}
> \text{Círculo: }\qquad &S=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{r}r&d r\ d \theta\qquad &=\pi r^2 \\
> \text{Esfera: }\qquad &S=\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}r^2\sin (\phi)&d \phi\ d \theta\qquad &=4\pi r^2 \\
> \text{Cilindro: }\qquad &S=\int_{0}^{h}\int_{0}^{2\pi}R&d \theta\ d z\qquad &=2\pi rh
>\end{align}$$
>

### Distribución de Carga Volumétrica 
$$Q=\rho V\qquad d q=\rho \cdot d V$$
>[!info] Volúmenes $\Omega=\iiint_{V} d V$
>  $$\begin{align}
>  \text{Esfera: }\qquad &\Omega=\int_{0}^{\pi}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{r}r^2\sin(\phi)&d r\ d \theta\ d \phi\qquad&=\frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 \\
>  \text{Cilindro: }\qquad&\Omega=\int_{0}^{h}\int_{0}^{2\pi}\int_{0}^{r}r&d r\ d \theta\ d z\qquad&=\pi r^2h
>  \end{align}$$
